Your money is protected by the Financial Claims Scheme

All deposit-based products listed on Meridian Finance are offered by APRA-authorised banks and credit unions. Eligible deposits are protected by Australia's Financial Claims Scheme (FCS) up to A$250,000 per institution — so your capital is safe even if a provider fails.
